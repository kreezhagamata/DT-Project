What You Can Do To Help the Planet

At Home

- Unplug chargers and devices.
- Reduce, reuse, recycle.
- Turn off the lights and appliances when not in use.

At School

- Do not litter at school
- Pick up rubbish if you see one
- Use reusable lunch boxes and water bottles.

In Your Community

- Join clean-up events
- Plant trees or plants
- Spread awareness

- Save as much water as you can
- Walk or bike instead of using a car
- Avoid single-use plastic for the day
